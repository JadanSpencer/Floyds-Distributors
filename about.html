Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leaders in food packaging distribution

Founded in 1995 by Robert Floyd, Floyd's Distributors began as a small family business with a single warehouse in Portland, Oregon. What started as a local supplier of basic food containers has grown into a nationally recognized distributor of premium packaging solutions.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic partnerships with manufacturers, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We've expanded our product lines to include eco-friendly options and custom branding services, always staying ahead of industry trends.

Today, with over 25 years in business, we serve thousands of restaurants, catering companies, and food service businesses across the country, while maintaining the personal touch that made us successful in the first place.
